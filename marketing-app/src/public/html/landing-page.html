Program Overview

Vector is a 3-year program. Applicants to Vector apply alongside their college applications.

1

High School Junior

Candidates must complete key coursework so that they are prepared for the engineering workplace.

2

High School Senior

Candidates apply to Vector and interview with Vector Companies. Admissions are available on both early and regular timelines

3

Junior Apprentice I

The first year of training experience. Apprenctices assume a junior role on the engineering team and determine their preferred concentration.

4

Junior Apprentice II

Apprentices assume graduated responsibility within their chosen concentration.

5

Senior Apprentice

Apprentices progress towards the responsibilities of a full-time Junior Engineer and pass the Fundamentals of Engineering examination.

Application Process

Vector matches top high school seniors to top engineering companies. After two rounds of interviews, our algorithm will create a match based on each parties' preferences.

1

Apply

After completing all the required AP classes as well as the SAT, apply to Vector in the Fall of your senior year of high school.

2

Interview

Vector will screen for the top applicants and coordinate interviews with all the hiring companies to which you applied.

3

Rank

After completing your interviews, come up with a list ranking your top companies. Companies will do the same for their top applicants.

4

Match

Our algorithm will match an applicant to a company based on each parties rank lists. Once you've matched, you're offically hired.
