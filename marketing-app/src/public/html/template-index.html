Program Overview

Vector is a 3-year program. Applicants to Vector apply alongside their college applications.

1

High School Junior

Candidates must complete key coursework so that they are prepared for the engineering workplace.

2

High School Senior

Candidates apply to Vector and interview with Vector Companies. Admissions are available on both early and regular timelines.

3

Junior Apprentice I

The first year of training experience. Apprenctices assume a junior role on the engineering team and determine their preferred concentration.

4

Junior Apprentice II

Apprentices assume graduated responsibility within their chosen concentration.

5

Senior Apprentice

Apprentices progress towards the responsibilities of a full-time Junior Engineer and pass the Fundamentals of Engineering examination.
