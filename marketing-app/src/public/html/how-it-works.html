Application Process

Vector matches top high school seniors to top engineering companies. After two rounds of interviews, our algorithm will create a match based on each parties' preferences.

1

Apply

After completing all the required AP classes as well as the SAT, apply to Vector in the Fall of your senior year of high school.

2

Interview

Vector will screen for the top applicants and coordinate interviews with all the hiring companies to which you applied.

3

Rank

After completing your interviews, come up with a list ranking your top companies. Companies will do the same for their top applicants.

4

Match

Our algorithm will match an applicant to a company based on each parties rank lists. Once you've matched, you're offically hired.
